Michael McClurkin, MD, MPP

I am a resident physician in Yale University's Department of Psychiatry. My previous research covered a wide range of topics, including community-based interventions to optimize the use of web-based and wearable technology to improve cardiovascular health, and psychosocial epidemiology.

I graduated with a BA in Biological Sciences (2013) from State University of New York at Buffalo, a Master of Public Policy (2019) from Harvard Kennedy School, and Doctor of Medicine (2019) from Harvard Medical School.
